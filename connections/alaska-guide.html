Our growing guide to Alaska — from the Arctic Circle to the Homer Spit, 3,000+ miles of roads, glaciers, mountain passes, wildlife, small towns, and one of the greatest adventures in the 3Dudes1Life story.

Why This Guide Exists

Alaska is one of the biggest adventures we have ever taken together. What started as a birthday celebration became a massive road trip across America's last frontier, covering more than 3,000 miles from the Arctic Circle in the north to the Homer Spit on the Kenai Peninsula.

This guide will become a place to organize the route, favorite stops, photos, travel notes, blog links, podcast memories, and the stories that still come up in our conversations today.
