🏔️ Alaska Guide

Our growing guide to Alaska — from Anchorage to the Arctic Circle, Coldfoot, the Brooks Range, glaciers, mountain passes, wildlife, small towns, and one of the greatest adventures in the 3Dudes1Life story.

Why This Guide Exists

Alaska is one of the biggest adventures we have ever taken together. What started as a birthday celebration became a massive road trip across America's last frontier, covering thousands of miles from Anchorage through Fairbanks, the Dalton Highway, the Arctic Circle, Coldfoot, and deep into Alaska’s northern wilderness.

This guide is starting as a real interactive route map with the major places we visited, drove through, stayed near, or photographed. Over time, we can add more exact stops, photos, ratings, travel notes, blog links, podcast memories, and the stories that still come up in our conversations today.

🏔️ Trip Snapshot

🛣️ Route

Anchorage to Palmer, Wasilla, Denali, Fairbanks, Coldfoot, the Brooks Range, and back south through more glacier country.

⭕ Furthest North

Past the Arctic Circle, through Coldfoot, and into tundra and mountain country most travelers never reach.

❄️ Big Moments

Glaciers, mountain passes, remote roads, wildlife sightings, Arctic scenery, and some of the wildest drives of our lives.

⭐ Overall Vibe

Massive, remote, emotional, dramatic, beautiful, and completely unforgettable.
