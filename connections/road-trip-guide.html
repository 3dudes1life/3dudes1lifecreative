Why This Guide Exists

Road trips have always been part of how we explore, reset, reconnect, laugh, argue, find random food stops, and create stories together. Some of our best memories happened between destinations — in the car, on the side of the road, at weird stops, and during the long drives that turned into their own adventures.

This guide will become a place to organize our road trip memories, favorite routes, photo stops, travel notes, podcast stories, and the drives that connect different parts of the 3Dudes1Life universe.
