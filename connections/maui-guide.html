🌴 Maui Guide

Our growing guide to Maui — favorite beaches, whale watching adventures, scenic drives, sunsets, local food, unforgettable views, and the places that keep bringing us back to the Valley Isle.

Why This Guide Exists

Maui has been part of our story for years. From incredible sunsets and whale watching adventures to favorite restaurants, beaches, scenic drives, and unforgettable ocean views, Maui continues to be one of our favorite places in Hawaii.

This guide will become a collection of places we have personally visited, loved, photographed, and recommended. Rather than a generic travel guide, this will be Maui through the eyes of 3Dudes1Life.

🍍 Food + Local Favorites

Restaurants, food trucks, coffee stops, shave ice, and local spots we would recommend.

Waterfalls, overlooks, roadside stops, and adventures beyond the resort areas.

Personal photos, favorite views, travel notes, and visual memories from our Maui trips.

🎙️ Podcast + Blog Connections

Stories connected to the podcast, blog archive, travel memories, and future creative projects.

Version 1 of our Maui map featuring real locations from our trips including Lahaina, Wailea, Road to Hana, waterfalls, beaches, restaurants, scenic overlooks, and some of our favorite Maui memories.

Where This Connects

The Maui guide will connect into the podcast, blog archive, travel memories, photo collections, island recommendations, and future creative projects tied to some of our favorite Hawaii experiences.

Over time, this page can become the Maui hub inside 3Dudes1Life Creative — starting simple now, then growing into a full island guide with photos, stories, favorite stops, maps, and recommendations.
