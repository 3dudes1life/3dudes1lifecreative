🌴 Maui Guide

Our growing guide to Maui — favorite beaches, whale watching adventures, scenic drives, sunsets, local food, unforgettable views, and the places that keep bringing us back to the Valley Isle.

Why This Guide Exists

Maui has been part of our story for years. From incredible sunsets and whale watching adventures to favorite restaurants, beaches, scenic drives, and unforgettable ocean views, Maui continues to be one of our favorite places in Hawaii.

This guide will become a collection of places we have personally visited, loved, photographed, and recommended. Rather than a generic travel guide, this will be Maui through the eyes of 3Dudes1Life.

What's Included in This Guide

🏖️ Beaches + Coastal Adventures

Explore some of our favorite Maui beaches including Big Beach, Little Beach, North Shore Baby Beach, Ho‘okipa Beach Park, Maluaka Beach, Waiehu Beach, and the famous black sands of Waiʻānapanapa.

🐢 Sea Turtles + Ocean Life

Discover Maui's best turtle viewing locations, snorkeling spots, protected reefs, ocean overlooks, and wildlife experiences we encountered during our travels.

🍍 Restaurants + Local Favorites

From local hidden gems to Road to Hana dining stops, we've included some of our favorite places to eat, snack, and enjoy authentic Maui flavors.

🚗 Road to Hana + Scenic Drives

Follow our journey along the Road to Hana, the Pipiwai Trail, waterfalls, black sand beaches, Hana Town, and the unforgettable backside of Haleakalā.

🥾 Hikes + Waterfalls

Experience some of Maui's most incredible outdoor adventures including Makamakaole Stream Trail, Makahiku Falls, Waimoku Falls, and the famous bamboo forest of the Pipiwai Trail.

📸 Interactive Travel Map

Every pin includes our personal photos, travel notes, tips, GPS locations, recommendations, and memories from places we've actually visited across Maui.

🗺️ Interactive Maui Adventure Map

Version 3 of our Maui map featuring real locations from our trips including Lahaina, Wailea, Road to Hana, waterfalls, beaches, restaurants, scenic overlooks, and some of our favorite Maui memories.

Where This Connects

Maui is more than just a destination for us — it has become part of our personal story. The island connects directly to our real-life travels, photography, podcast conversations, blog archive, and many of the memories that continue to shape 3Dudes1Life Creative.

Maui also plays a role in Tres Amigos, Una Vida – A Throuple Love Story, where experiences, locations, and inspiration from our travels help influence the world and adventures found throughout the series. Many of the places featured on this map represent locations we have personally visited, photographed, and experienced together.

This guide serves as our growing Maui hub — combining travel stories, interactive maps, local recommendations, photography, hiking adventures, beaches, restaurants, and future memories as we continue exploring one of our favorite islands in Hawaiʻi.
