🌺 Big Island Guide

Our growing guide to the Big Island — favorite stops, food, coffee, beaches, volcano moments, Hilo-side adventures, and the places we would actually recommend from our 3Dudes1Life travels.

Why This Guide Exists

The Big Island is becoming one of the most important places in the 3Dudes1Life universe. This guide will grow into a living travel resource built from places we have personally visited, loved, photographed, researched, or would recommend to someone following our journey.

This is not about sharing private details. This is about sharing the adventure: the roads, beaches, food stops, coffee runs, volcanic landscapes, small towns, local gems, and memories that make the Big Island feel unforgettable.

What’s Inside The Guide

☕ Food + Coffee

Restaurants, smoothie stops, grocery runs, local food finds, and places we actually tried while exploring the island.

🏖️ Beaches + Coastlines

Black sand beaches, lava cliffs, ocean overlooks, sea arches, surf spots, and dramatic shoreline views.

🌋 Volcano + Lava Landscapes

Volcanoes National Park, lava tubes, crater overlooks, lava fields, eruption history, and places where the island is still changing.

🌈 3Dudes Travel Notes

Our honest ratings, personal vibe checks, LGBTQ+ comfort notes, favorite stops, and places we would recommend from our own experience.

🚗 Scenic Drives + Road Stops

Jungle roads, pull-offs, scenic routes, small towns, historic roads, and easy stops worth adding to a Big Island day trip.

📸 Photo Memories

Photos from our own visits, favorite views, travel moments, and visual highlights connected to each map pin.

🗺️ Interactive Big Island Adventure Map

Version 2 of our Big Island map featuring volcanoes, lava coastlines, beaches, food stops, photo locations, and favorite places we've explored across East Hawaiʻi.

Where This Connects

The Big Island guide will connect into the podcast, book universe, blog archive, photo memories, travel recommendations, and future island-based creative projects.

Over time, this page can become the first real “map-style” experience inside 3Dudes1Life Creative — starting simple now, then growing into something more interactive as more photos, pins, stories, and recommendations are added.
